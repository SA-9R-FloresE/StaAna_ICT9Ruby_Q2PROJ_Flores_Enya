Care for a snack?

Cassava Cake

2 cups grated, peeled yuca

1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk

1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk

1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk

2 large eggs, beaten

Ingredients

1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

2. Stir yuca, coconut milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk, and eggs together in a bowl until thoroughly combined.

3. Pour into a 2-quart baking dish.

4. Bake in the preheated oven until set, about 1 hour. Turn the broiler on and bake until top of cake is browned, 2 to 3 minutes. Cool completely in the refrigerator before serving, about 1 hour.


Hungry for breakfast?

Chicken Adobo

2 tablespoons vegetable oil

1 whole chicken, cut into parts

4 cloves garlic

1 onion, chunked

5 leaf bay leaves

1/2 cup light brown sugar

1 cup low sodium soy sauce

1 cup white vinegar

2 cups water

8 black peppercorns

steamed rice for serving

Ingredients

1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

2. Add garlic, onion, bay leaves, brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, water, and peppercorns. Stir and bring to a nice simmer.

3. Cover with lid and simmer over medium heat for 15 minutes. Remove the lid and simmer for another 15 minutes.

4. Serve with rice!

Maybe a beverage?

Sago't Gulaman

1 cup tapioca pearls

1/2 oz red gelatin powder (unflavored)

6 cups water for boiling

2 tablespoons granulated white sugar

1 cup brown sugar

1 cup water

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1. Cook the tapioca by boiling 4 cups of water in a pot. Add the tapioca pearls. Boil for 8 minutes in medium heat setting. Reduce the heat to the lowest setting and then continue boiling until the pearls turn translucent and chewy. Set it aside.

2. Cook the gelatin by boiling 2 cups of water in a pot. Add white sugar and gelatin powder. Turn the heat off and stir. Transfer the mixture to a glass container. Let it cool down. Refrigerate until the gelatin sets.

3. Make the syrup by combining all the syrup ingredients in a cooking pot. Let it boil. Cook for 1 minute while stirring. Set aside.

4. In a glass, combine some tapioca pearls, sliced gelatin, 3 tablespoons of syrup , and a cup of cold water. Stir.

5. Serve. Share and enjoy!
